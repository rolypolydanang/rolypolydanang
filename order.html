Choose Your Sauce

Every spring roll comes with your choice of our house-made sauces — crafted fresh daily with local ingredients.

  • Fish Sauce (GF) — our signature blend of sweet, salty, and tangy
  • Peanut (V) — creamy and rich, a perfect classic
  • Sweet & Sour (GF) — bright and balanced with a hint of chili
  • Toasted Sesame (V) — nutty, fragrant, and smooth
  • Spicy Mayo — creamy with a touch of heat

V = Vegan | GF = Gluten-Free
