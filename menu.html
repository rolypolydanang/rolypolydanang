Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes. Our Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado roll is vegan and gluten-free, and several fillings are gluten-free. Peanut & Toasted Sesame sauces are vegan; Fish Sauce is gluten-free. Gluten-free? Please tell our staff and we’ll omit the fried shallots (they contain gluten).

Which spring roll fillings are premium?

Garlic Prawn (+10,000₫), Lemongrass Beef (+5,000₫) and Smoked Goose (+5,000₫) are premium fillings. You can mix fillings in any set.

How do I order gluten-free spring rolls?

Please let our staff know when ordering. We’ll prepare your rolls gluten-free and leave out the fried shallots, which contain gluten.

What grade is your matcha and where is it from?

We use ceremonial-grade matcha sourced directly from Japan. Matcha lattes are made with oat milk by default.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes—free within 3 km in An Thuong (Ngu Hanh Son) at in-store prices. Order by phone at +84 327 306 076 or via GrabFood.

What are your hours?

Mon–Sat 10:00–20:00. Closed Sunday.
