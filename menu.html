House-Made Spring Roll Dipping Sauces — Roly Poly Đà Nẵng

Trio of Signature Vietnamese Sauces

  • Peanut Sauce (Vegan) — Creamy, rich, and slightly sweet; made with roasted peanuts and coconut milk for an authentic Vietnamese gỏi cuốn dip.
  • Toasted Sesame Sauce (Vegan) — Deep nutty flavor with a smooth, savory finish, perfect for pairing with fresh herbs and rice paper rolls.
  • Vietnamese Fish Sauce (Gluten-free) — Classic nước chấm with a balance of sweet, tangy, and umami-rich flavors from garlic, lime, and chili.

All dipping sauces are made fresh in-house daily for maximum flavor and quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We’re open Monday to Saturday, 10:00–20:00. We’re closed on Sunday.

Do you offer delivery in Da Nang?

Yes. Free delivery within 3 km of An Thuong / My Khe. Call us or see delivery details. You can also order on GrabFood.

How much are the spring rolls?

Sets from 105k (3 rolls). Singles 40k; Garlic Prawn +10k, Smoked Goose or Lemongrass Beef +5k. See the full spring roll menu.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes. Our Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado rolls are vegan and gluten-free, and many fillings are gluten-free. Peanut and Sesame sauces are vegan; fish sauce is gluten-free—see our dipping sauces.

Do you serve Vietnamese salted coffee (cà phê muối)?

Yes. We make iced salted coffee with Vietnamese phin coffee and a silky salted cream foam. Check our coffee & matcha menu.

Where are you located?

05 An Thượng 6, Bắc Mỹ Phú, Ngũ Hành Sơn, Đà Nẵng — 3 minutes from An Thuong Night Market and near My Khe Beach. Get directions.

Do you accept card or mobile payments?

Yes. We accept Cash, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, and MoMo.

Do I need a reservation?

No reservation needed. Walk-ins, takeaway, and delivery are available. Contact us if you’re a large group.

Are your sauces vegan or gluten-free?

Peanut and Toasted Sesame sauces are vegan; Vietnamese fish sauce (nước chấm) is gluten-free. All are made in-house daily—see sauces.

Do you have an English menu?

Yes. Our menu is available in English and Vietnamese. View the online menu or download the PDF.
