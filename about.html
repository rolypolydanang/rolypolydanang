Our Story — Vietnamese Spring Roll Café in An Thượng

Born from a love of Vietnamese street food, Roly Poly focuses on healthy, flavorful food that travels well — perfect for lunch, beach picnics, or a relaxed dine-in experience.

Every roll is layered with rice vermicelli, crisp lettuce, carrot, cucumber, mint & coriander, then finished with fillings like garlic prawn, lemongrass beef, or tofu, mushroom & avocado.

Complete your meal with house-made sauces, Vietnamese coffee & matcha, and fresh juices.

Our Philosophy

  • Made-to-order gỏi cuốn.
  • House-made sauces — peanut, toasted sesame & nước chấm.
  • Specialty coffee, matcha & juices.
  • Locally sourced produce whenever possible.
  • Friendly service near Mỹ Khê Beach.
