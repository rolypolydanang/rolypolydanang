Our rolls are hand-rolled to order with local herbs and seasonal produce, and our drinks menu reflects what we love: Vietnamese phin coffee, fresh juices and matcha lattes. Our Story

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang is a neighborhood spot built around a simple idea: great ingredients, prepared with care.

We fell in love with Vietnamese street-food traditions—especially gỏi cuốn—for their balance, freshness and portability. We started small in An Thuong, close to the sea, and kept things personal: rolls made to order, polite service, and a space that invites you to slow down and stay awhile.

Every roll begins with crisp herbs and greens, rice vermicelli and our house touches— then we add your preferred filling and roll everything in soft rice paper. On the drinks side, we brew phin coffee in the Vietnamese style and whisk ceremonial-grade matcha sourced directly from Japan.

  • Made-to-order: We hand-roll so everything tastes bright and fresh.
  • Thoughtful sourcing: We buy herbs and vegetables locally whenever possible.
  • Clear, kind hospitality: Friendly staff, Wi-Fi, and a calm place to recharge.

Whether you’re dropping in after a swim, meeting friends, or grabbing something to go, we’re here to make choosing lunch simple—and delicious.

Our Philosophy — Fresh, Local Vietnamese Restaurant & Coffee Shop in Da Nang

Ingredients & Sourcing

Good food starts with good ingredients. We keep our list short and our standards high.

  • Herbs & greens: Sourced locally when possible for maximum freshness.
  • Proteins: Prepared in small batches for clean, consistent flavor.
  • Matcha: Ceremonial-grade, sourced directly from Japan.
  • Coffee: Brewed in the traditional phin style.

How We Roll

  1. Prep: Wash and chill herbs and greens; cook noodles; prepare proteins.
  2. Layer: Rice paper, rice vermicelli, crisp vegetables, fresh herbs, chosen filling.
  3. Roll: Tight enough to hold together, gentle enough to keep textures lively.
  4. Serve: Pair with a house-made dipping sauce—peanut, toasted sesame or fish sauce.

Dietary Needs

We’re happy to help with dietary requests. Gluten-free? Please tell our team when ordering— we’ll omit the fried shallots (they contain gluten). Vegan options are available.

Our Space

A calm, cozy room in An Thuong with natural light, Wi-Fi and a few quiet corners. Come by after a swim, bring a book, or settle in with a coffee and your laptop.

FAQ

Why gỏi cuốn—and why the name “Roly Poly”?

We love the balance and portability of gỏi cuốn—fresh herbs, vegetables, noodles and protein in one tidy roll. “Roly Poly” is a playful nod to the rolling technique and to keeping things simple and fun.

Are your spring rolls made to order?

Yes. We hand-roll to order so everything tastes bright and fresh, with herbs and greens prepped throughout the day.

What’s typically inside a roll?

Rice vermicelli, crisp lettuce, carrot, cucumber, fresh mint and coriander—then your chosen filling—rolled in soft rice paper. Pair with a house-made sauce.

How do you source ingredients?

We buy herbs and produce locally in Da Nang whenever possible and prep proteins in small batches for clean, consistent flavor.

What sauces do you make in-house?

Peanut, toasted sesame and classic nước chấm (Vietnamese fish sauce)—crafted to complement the rolls without overpowering them.

Can you accommodate gluten-free or vegan?

Yes. Please tell our team when ordering. We can make rolls gluten-free (we’ll omit fried shallots, which contain gluten) and we have several vegan options.

What about your drinks—coffee and matcha?

We brew Vietnamese phin coffee and whisk ceremonial-grade matcha sourced directly from Japan—simple drinks we love, prepared with care.

Is the café laptop-friendly? Do you take reservations?

Yes—there’s Wi-Fi and a few quiet corners. We’re casual and walk-in friendly; for groups over 6, please call +84 327 306 076 and we’ll try to arrange seating.
