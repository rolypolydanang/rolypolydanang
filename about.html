Our Story — Fresh Vietnamese Spring Rolls (gỏi cuốn) in Da Nang

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang is a specialty Vietnamese spring roll restaurant & café in the heart of An Thuong, near My Khe Beach. We hand-roll gỏi cuốn to order with locally sourced herbs and vegetables, quality proteins, and house-made dipping sauces.

We started Roly Poly from a love of authentic Vietnamese street food and a simple idea: healthy, flavorful food that travels well — whether you’re grabbing lunch, taking rolls to the beach, or dining in our cozy, vintage-inspired café. Explore our spring roll fillings and Vietnamese coffee & matcha.

Each rice-paper roll is layered with rice vermicelli, crisp lettuce, carrot, cucumber, fresh mint, and coriander, then finished with proteins like garlic prawn, lemongrass beef, or tofu, mushroom & avocado (vegan & gluten-free). Pair with our peanut, toasted sesame, or classic nước chấm (Vietnamese fish) sauces.

We’re proud to support the Da Nang community by buying from nearby growers whenever possible. Come for friendly service, fresh tropical juices, and your new favorite rolls — or opt for free delivery within 3 km.

Our Philosophy — Fresh, Local Vietnamese Café in Da Nang

  • Made-to-order Vietnamese spring rolls (gỏi cuốn) — see fillings & pricing.

  • House-made sauces to complement every roll — peanut, toasted sesame & nước chấm.

  • Specialty Vietnamese coffee, premium matcha & fresh juices — explore coffee & matcha and juices.

  • Locally sourced produce from Da Nang whenever possible — fresher flavor, lower food miles.

  • Friendly service in our cozy, vintage café in An Thuong, near My Khe Beach — dine-in, takeaway, or free delivery within 3 km.
