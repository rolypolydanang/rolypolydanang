Our Story — Fresh Vietnamese Spring Rolls (Gỏi cuốn) in Đà Nẵng

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang is a specialty fresh spring roll restaurant & Vietnamese café in the heart of An Thượng, a beachside neighborhood near Mỹ Khê Beach. We hand-roll gỏi cuốn to order with locally sourced herbs and vegetables, quality proteins, and house-made dipping sauces.

We started Roly Poly from a love of authentic Vietnamese street food and a simple idea: healthy, flavorful food that travels well — whether you’re grabbing lunch, taking rolls to the beach, or dining in our cozy, vintage-inspired café. Explore our spring roll fillings and Vietnamese coffee & matcha.

Each rice-paper roll is layered with rice vermicelli, crisp lettuce, carrot, cucumber, fresh mint, and coriander, then finished with proteins like garlic prawn, lemongrass beef, or tofu, mushroom & avocado (vegan & gluten-free). Pair with our peanut, toasted sesame, or classic nước chấm sauces.

We’re proud to support the Đà Nẵng community by buying from nearby growers whenever possible. Come for friendly service, fresh tropical juices, and your new favorite rolls — or opt for free delivery within 3 km.
