About Roly Poly Da Nang

Fresh spring rolls, house-made sauces, and a cozy neighborhood spot in An Thượng.

We started Roly Poly with a simple idea: great ingredients, rolled with care. Here’s how we make gỏi cuốn that travel well, taste bright, and keep you coming back.

Our Story Ingredients FAQs

Our Story

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang is a small, independent spot a short walk from My Khe Beach. We focus on fresh gỏi cuốn and simple drinks done right.

We fell in love with Vietnamese street-food traditions—especially gỏi cuốn—for their balance, texture, and portability. We kept the concept honest and personal: rolled to order, friendly service, and a space that invites you to slow down.

Every roll starts with crisp herbs and greens, rice vermicelli, and your choice of filling, wrapped in soft rice paper. For drinks, we brew Vietnamese phin coffee and whisk ceremonial-grade matcha—nothing fussy, just really good.

  • Made to order: Rolls are hand-rolled so they arrive bright and fresh.
  • Local first: We source herbs and produce in Da Nang whenever possible.
  • Kind hospitality: Calm room, Wi-Fi, and friendly staff.

Swing by after a swim, meet a friend, or take rolls to the beach—we’ll keep lunch easy.

What We Stand For

  • Fresh & balanced. Herbs, vegetables, noodles, and protein in every bite.

  • Made here. House-made sauces that lift the roll, not overpower it.

  • Simple drinks we love. Vietnamese phin coffee and ceremonial-grade matcha.

  • Local sourcing. Da Nang herbs and produce whenever we can.

  • Everyone’s welcome. Travelers, nomads, and neighbors—come as you are.

Ingredients & Sourcing

Short list. High standards. Prepped throughout the day.

  • Herbs & greens: Sourced locally when possible for peak freshness.
  • Proteins: Prepared in small batches for clean, consistent flavor.
  • Matcha: Ceremonial-grade, sourced directly from Japan.
  • Coffee: Brewed in the traditional phin style.

How We Roll

  1. Prep: Wash and chill herbs and greens; cook noodles; marinate and cook proteins.
  2. Layer: Rice paper, vermicelli, crunchy veg, fresh herbs, chosen filling.
  3. Roll: Tight enough to hold; gentle enough to keep textures lively.
  4. Serve: Pair with your choice of house-made dipping sauce.

Dietary Needs

We can help with dietary requests. Gluten-free? Tell us at the counter— we’ll omit the fried shallots (they contain gluten). Several vegan options are available.

Our Space

A calm, sun-lit room in An Thượng with Wi-Fi and a few quiet corners. Drop in after the beach, bring a book, or settle in with a coffee and your laptop.

FAQ

Why gỏi cuốn—and why “Roly Poly”?

Gỏi cuốn packs herbs, veg, noodles, and protein into one fresh bite. “Roly Poly” nods to the rolling technique—and to keeping things light and fun.

Are the rolls made to order?

Yes. We hand-roll to order so everything stays bright and crisp.

What’s inside a typical roll?

Rice vermicelli, lettuce, carrot, cucumber, fresh mint and coriander, plus your chosen filling—wrapped in soft rice paper and served with a house sauce.

How do you source ingredients?

We buy herbs and produce locally in Da Nang whenever possible and prep proteins in small batches through the day.

Which sauces are house-made?

All of them—peanut, sweet & sour, spicy mayo, toasted sesame, and classic nước chấm. Crafted to complement, not overpower.

Gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes. For gluten-free we omit fried shallots; several fillings and sauces are vegan. Just let us know when ordering.

What about coffee and matcha?

We brew Vietnamese phin coffee and whisk ceremonial-grade matcha—simple drinks, prepared with care.

Laptop-friendly? Reservations?

Yes—Wi-Fi and a few quiet corners. We’re walk-in friendly; for groups of 6+ call +84 327 306 076.
