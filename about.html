Our Story – Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang

Da Nang’s first specialty spring roll café — fresh, local, and made with love in An Thuong.

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang is the city’s first specialty fresh spring roll restaurant and café, created to bring fresh, flavorful spring rolls with unique fillings and house-made sauces to the heart of Da Nang’s An Thuong area.

Our story began with a passion for Vietnamese street food and a dream to share fresh, healthy spring rolls with both locals and travelers. We couldn’t find a place focused on made-to-order spring rolls with high-quality ingredients, so we decided to create it ourselves.

Every roll we serve is a celebration of Vietnamese flavors and a commitment to quality. We hand-roll each one using fresh, locally sourced produce wherever possible, and pair it with our unique house-made sauces. Our cozy, vintage-inspired café is designed to make you feel welcome, whether you’re dropping in for lunch, grabbing takeaway, or relaxing with a coffee.

We believe Vietnamese food should be vibrant, healthy, and crafted with care — that’s why we keep our menu simple but full of flavor. Pair your rolls with our specialty Vietnamese salted coffee, coconut coffee, or a premium matcha latte for a perfect local experience.

We’re proud to be part of the Da Nang community and strive to support local farmers and suppliers whenever possible.
