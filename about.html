Our Story

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang is a specialty Vietnamese spring roll restaurant & café in the heart of An Thượng, near Mỹ Khê Beach. We hand-roll gỏi cuốn to order with locally sourced herbs and vegetables, quality proteins, and house-made dipping sauces.

Each rice-paper roll is layered with rice vermicelli, crisp lettuce, carrot, cucumber, fresh mint, and coriander, then finished with fillings like garlic prawn, lemongrass beef, or tofu, mushroom & avocado (vegan & gluten-free). Pair with our peanut, toasted sesame, or classic nước chấm sauces.

We also brew authentic Vietnamese phin coffee — try our salted coffee or coconut coffee — and make matcha lattes with oat milk and sugar-free fresh juices.

Open Mon–Sat, 10:00–20:00. Walk-ins welcome. Dine-in, takeaway, and GrabFood delivery available.
