About Roly Poly Da Nang

Fresh spring rolls, house-made sauces, and a relaxed beach-neighbourhood café.

We started Roly Poly with a simple idea: great ingredients, rolled with care. Every order is hand-rolled so the herbs stay crisp, the rice-paper stays soft, and the flavour stays bright—perfect for a light lunch, a beach takeaway, or a coffee break with friends.

Our Story

In a city of unforgettable food, we built a small spot devoted to one favourite: gỏi cuốn — fresh Vietnamese spring rolls.

We love the balance of textures and herbs you get in a great roll. So we kept our concept honest and focused: hand-rolled to order, house-made sauces, and simple drinks we actually crave — Vietnamese phin coffee and ceremonial-grade matcha.

You’ll find us a short walk from My Khe Beach on An Thượng 6. Drop in after a swim, meet a friend, or grab rolls to take to the sand — we’ll keep lunch easy.

  • Made fresh: Nothing sits. We prep throughout the day for peak crunch and colour.
  • Local first: Herbs and produce are sourced in Da Nang whenever possible.
  • Kind hospitality: Calm room, Wi-Fi, friendly team.

FAQ

Why gỏi cuốn—and why “Roly Poly”?

Gỏi cuốn brings herbs, veg, noodles and protein into one fresh bite. “Roly Poly” nods to the rolling and to keeping things light.

Are the rolls made to order?

Yes. We hand-roll to order so everything stays bright and crisp.

What’s inside a typical roll?

Rice vermicelli, lettuce, carrot, cucumber, fresh mint and coriander, plus your chosen filling—wrapped in soft rice paper and served with a house sauce.

How do you source ingredients?

We buy herbs and produce locally in Da Nang whenever possible and prep proteins in small batches through the day.

Which sauces are house-made?

All of them—peanut, sweet & sour, spicy mayo, toasted sesame, and classic nước chấm. Crafted to complement, not overpower.

Gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes. For gluten-free we omit fried shallots; several fillings and sauces are vegan. Just let us know when ordering.

What about coffee and matcha?

We brew Vietnamese phin coffee and whisk ceremonial-grade matcha—simple drinks, prepared with care.

Laptop-friendly? Reservations?

Yes—Wi-Fi and a few quiet corners. We’re walk-in friendly; for groups of 6+ call +84 327 306 076.
