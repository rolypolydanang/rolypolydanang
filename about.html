About Roly Poly Da Nang — Our Story & Values

A small, independent Vietnamese restaurant & café in An Thượng — steps from Mỹ Khê Beach

We opened Roly Poly to make fresh, feel-good food you can enjoy at the coffee shop or on the beach. Our rolls are hand-rolled to order with local herbs and seasonal produce, and our drinks menu reflects what we love: Vietnamese phin coffee, fresh juices and ceremonial-grade matcha lattes from Japan. It’s a relaxed, laptop-friendly space with Wi-Fi and friendly faces.

Our Story

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang is a neighborhood spot built around a simple idea: great ingredients, prepared with care.

We fell in love with Vietnamese street-food traditions—especially gỏi cuốn—for their balance, freshness and portability. We started small in An Thượng, close to the sea, and kept things personal: rolls made to order, polite service, and a space that invites you to slow down and stay awhile.

Every roll begins with crisp herbs and greens, rice vermicelli and our house touches— then we add your preferred filling and roll everything in soft rice paper. On the drinks side, we brew phin coffee in the Vietnamese style and whisk ceremonial-grade matcha sourced directly from Japan.

  • Made-to-order: We hand-roll so everything tastes bright and fresh.
  • Thoughtful sourcing: We buy herbs and vegetables locally whenever possible.
  • Clear, kind hospitality: Friendly staff, Wi-Fi, and a calm place to recharge.

Whether you’re dropping in after a swim, meeting friends, or grabbing something to go, we’re here to make choosing lunch simple—and delicious.

Our Philosophy — Fresh, Local Vietnamese Café in Da Nang

  • Fresh & balanced. Gỏi cuốn is all about texture and harmony—herbs, vegetables, noodles and protein in every bite.

  • Made here. Dipping sauces are crafted in-house to complement the rolls without overpowering them.

  • Simple drinks we love. Vietnamese phin coffee and ceremonial-grade matcha prepared with care.

  • Local first. We source herbs and produce in Da Nang whenever possible for peak freshness.

  • Everyone’s welcome. Friendly service, Wi-Fi, and a cozy space for travelers, nomads and neighbors.

Ingredients & Sourcing

Good food starts with good ingredients. We keep our list short and our standards high.

  • Herbs & greens: Sourced locally when possible for maximum freshness.
  • Proteins: Prepared in small batches for clean, consistent flavor.
  • Matcha: Ceremonial-grade, sourced directly from Japan.
  • Coffee: Brewed in the traditional phin style.

How We Roll

  1. Prep: Wash and chill herbs and greens; cook noodles; prepare proteins.
  2. Layer: Rice paper, rice vermicelli, crisp veg, fresh herbs, chosen filling.
  3. Roll: Tight enough to hold together, gentle enough to keep textures lively.
  4. Serve: Pair with a house-made dipping sauce—peanut, toasted sesame or fish sauce.

Dietary Needs

We’re happy to help with dietary requests. Gluten-free? Please tell our team when ordering— we’ll omit the fried shallots (they contain gluten). Vegan options are available.

Our Space

A calm, cozy room in An Thượng with natural light, Wi-Fi and a few quiet corners. Come by after a swim, bring a book, or settle in with a coffee and your laptop.
