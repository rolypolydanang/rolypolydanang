Getting Here — An Thuong / My An

  • From My Khe Beach: Walk ~5 minutes inland off Hoàng Kế Viêm; we’re three shops down from Cộng Cà Phê on An Thượng 6.
  • By Grab/Taxi: Search “Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang”.
  • By Motorbike: Turn onto An Thượng 6 from Hoàng Kế Viêm — free parking in front.
  • By Car: Short-term parking on side streets off An Thượng 6.
