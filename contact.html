FAQs — Visiting, Ordering & Delivery

Where is Roly Poly located in Đà Nẵng?

We’re at 05 An Thượng 6, Bắc Mỹ Phú, Ngũ Hành Sơn — a short walk from Mỹ Khê Beach. Tap the map or open Google Maps.

What are your opening hours?

Monday–Saturday: 10:00–20:00. Closed Sunday. For today’s hours, call +84 327 306 076.

Do you deliver in Đà Nẵng? Which areas?

Yes — free delivery within 3 km during opening hours (An Thượng, Mỹ An, Bắc Mỹ Phú). Call for in-store prices and fastest service: +84 327 306 076, or order on GrabFood.

How do I order pickup?

Call +84 327 306 076 and we’ll have your order ready. See fillings and prices on the spring rolls menu and drinks on coffee & matcha and juices.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free spring rolls?

Yes. We offer vegan and gluten-free options, including tofu, mushroom & avocado. Check our fillings and ask staff about allergens.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, MoMo, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay. Card payments have a 3% service fee. Holiday pricing: +10% on holidays; +20% during Tết.

Do you have dine-in seating?

Yes — cozy, vintage café seating. You can also takeaway or get delivery.

Can you handle larger/group orders?

We can prepare larger orders with notice. Please call +84 327 306 076 and we’ll advise prep time.
