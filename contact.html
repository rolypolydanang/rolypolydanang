Getting Here — An Thuong / My An

  • From My Khe Beach: Walk about 5 minutes inland from the beach, just off Hoàng Kế Viêm main street. We’re about three shops down from Cộng Cà Phê, at the corner of An Thượng 6 and Hoàng Kế Viêm.
  • By Grab or Taxi: Search for “Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang” — the address auto-fills correctly in most ride-hailing apps.
  • By Motorbike: From Hoàng Kế Viêm, turn onto An Thượng 6. Free scooter parking is available directly in front of the shop.
  • By Car: Short-term car parking is available on nearby side streets off An Thượng 6. Follow Google Maps for the fastest route.

Dine-In · Pickup · Delivery

Enjoy your favorite Vietnamese spring rolls, coffee, and juices however you like — relax in our cozy An Thượng café, pick up your order on the way to My Khe Beach, or have it delivered right to your door.
