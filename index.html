Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

We’re at 05 An Thượng 6, Ngũ Hành Sơn, Đà Nẵng — a 5-minute walk from Mỹ Khê Beach. Get directions on Google Maps.

What are your opening hours?

Mon–Sat 10:00–20:00. Closed Sunday.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes — GrabFood and phone orders. We deliver within ~3 km of An Thượng. Call +84 327 306 076.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes — try our Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado rolls (vegan, GF). Peanut & sesame sauces are vegan; fish sauce is gluten-free. Ask our team if you have allergies.

What should I try first?

Best-sellers: Garlic Prawn or Lemongrass Beef spring rolls, plus our Salted Coffee or Coconut Coffee.

Do you take reservations?

We’re casual dine-in — no reservations needed. For groups (>6), call +84 327 306 076 and we’ll try to arrange seating.

Do you accept card payments?

Yes — Cash, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay and MoMo. Currency: VND.

Can you customize rolls (no chili / extra herbs)?

Absolutely — rolls are made to order. Tell us your preferences (mild, extra herbs, no peanuts, etc.).

Is there Wi-Fi and are you tourist friendly?

Yes — free Wi-Fi. We speak English & Vietnamese. Our menu is bilingual.

Is the café child-friendly or pet-friendly?

Kids welcome. Small, well-behaved pets are fine on the outdoor seating area.

Price range?

Most spring rolls are affordable — see menu & prices.
