About Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang is the city’s first specialty café dedicated to fresh spring rolls, also known as rice paper rolls or summer rolls — all handmade to order with high-quality, locally sourced ingredients. We offer vegan-friendly and gluten-free options, perfect for health-conscious travelers and locals alike.

Located in the heart of An Thuong, just minutes from My Khe Beach, our cozy vintage-style café is a favorite for anyone looking for healthy Vietnamese food in Da Nang. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, comfortable indoor seating, and plenty of Instagram-worthy vibes.

Each roll is served with our unique, house-made dipping sauces. Pair your meal with a signature drink: Vietnamese salted coffee, coconut coffee, matcha lattes, or refreshing fresh juices.
