About Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang is the city’s first specialty café dedicated to authentic Vietnamese spring rolls — also known as gỏi cuốn, rice paper rolls, or summer rolls. Every roll is handmade to order using high-quality, locally sourced ingredients. We offer vegan-friendly and gluten-free options, making us a go-to spot for health-conscious travelers, expats, and locals.

Our café is located in the heart of An Thuong — Da Nang’s vibrant tourist area — just minutes from My Khe Beach. Whether you’re visiting after a day in the sun or exploring the city, Roly Poly is the perfect place for healthy Vietnamese food in a cozy, vintage-inspired setting. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, comfortable indoor seating, and plenty of Instagram-worthy photo spots.

Each roll comes with our house-made dipping sauces, crafted to pair perfectly with your filling of choice. Complete your meal with a signature drink like Vietnamese salted coffee, iced coconut coffee, ceremonial-grade matcha lattes, or fresh tropical juices.
