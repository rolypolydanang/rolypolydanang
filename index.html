FAQs — Fresh Spring Rolls & Drinks

What is gỏi cuốn (fresh spring rolls)?

Soft rice paper wrapped around rice vermicelli, crisp greens, fresh herbs, and your chosen filling — dipped in a house-made sauce.

Are your rolls made to order?

Yes. We hand-roll to order so everything tastes bright and fresh.

Which sauce should I pick?

Peanut (classic & creamy), Toasted Sesame (nutty, vegan), or nước chấm (Vietnamese fish sauce — light & tangy).

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes. Vegan fillings include Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado. Nước chấm is gluten-free; Peanut & Toasted Sesame are vegan. Tell us if you need gluten-free and we’ll omit fried shallots.

What are your best-sellers?

Garlic Prawn and Lemongrass Beef spring rolls — plus Salted Coffee and Coconut Coffee.

How can I order?

Dine-in or takeaway, order via GrabFood, or call +84 327 306 076.
