Healthy Vietnamese Spring Rolls & Coffee in Da Nang

Handmade gỏi cuốn (rice paper rolls / summer rolls) with vegan, vegetarian & gluten-free options — in An Thượng (My An) near Mỹ Khê Beach. Open Mon–Sat for brunch, lunch & dinner.

We specialize in healthy, handmade spring rolls with a wide variety of fillings and house-made sauces. Pair your rolls with authentic Vietnamese coffee — including salted coffee and coconut coffee — plus matcha lattes and sugar-free fresh juices. Dine-in, takeaway, and GrabFood delivery available.
