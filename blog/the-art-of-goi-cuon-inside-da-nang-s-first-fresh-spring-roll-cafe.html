The Art of Gỏi Cuốn: Inside Da Nang’s First Fresh Spring Roll Café

August 15, 2025

Gỏi cuốn — fresh Vietnamese spring rolls — are simple at a glance: herbs, vegetables, noodles, and protein wrapped in soft rice paper. But behind every perfect bite is a quiet craft: balancing textures, seasoning fillings, and rolling with just the right tension so each roll is soft, sturdy, and bright with herbs.

A Taste of Da Nang’s Café Culture

Da Nang’s food scene is evolving fast, and fresh, light meals are in demand. We opened Roly Poly to bring a café-style experience to gỏi cuốn: made-to-order rolls, curated drinks, and a relaxed, beach-adjacent vibe in An Thượng.

Rolling Matters: Texture & Technique

  • Soft—but not soggy—rice paper: We hydrate just enough so it stretches without tearing.
  • Balanced fillings: Crisp herbs and cucumber for lift, protein for body, noodles for comfort.
  • Even pressure: A tight first tuck keeps everything aligned, then a gentle roll seals it.

Fresh, Every Time

We only roll when you order. That’s how the rice paper stays supple, herbs stay crisp, and the first bite “snaps” with freshness. It’s also why our rolls travel well for a beach walk or a quick lunch.

The Right Sauce for the Right Roll

Sauces aren’t an afterthought in Vietnamese food — they complete the dish. At Roly Poly, we make three sauces daily:

  • Nước chấm (gluten-free): Tangy, light, and aromatic — perfect with prawn and satay chicken.
  • Peanut sauce (vegan): Creamy and comforting — great with tofu & avocado or poached chicken.
  • Toasted sesame (vegan): Nutty and mellow — ideal with smoked goose or mushroom.

From Market to Café

We source fresh herbs and produce daily and prep in small batches to keep flavors bright. Proteins are marinated and cooked to highlight natural texture — never masked with heavy sauces.

FAQ

Are your rolls gluten-free?

Five of our seven rolls are naturally gluten-free. Peanut and sesame sauces contain soy sauce (gluten), while nước chấm is gluten-free.

Can I mix sauces?

Absolutely — you can order any roll with any sauce, and we’re happy to add fresh chili on request.

Do you make the rolls spicy?

Our default is mild; we can add fresh chili to peanut sauce or nước chấm. Sesame sauce is always mild.
