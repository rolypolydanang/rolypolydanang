Where to Eat After My Khe Beach | Roly Poly

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang

Salt on your skin, sand between your toes, and a rumbling stomach — that’s the My Khe Beach trifecta. After a swim or surf session, you want something fresh, fast, and not too heavy. A short stroll inland to the An Thuong area brings you to Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang, where made-to-order Vietnamese spring rolls and icy drinks revive you without slowing you down.

Why Spring Rolls Hit the Spot After the Beach

Vietnamese gỏi cuốn are engineered for hot-weather eating. Soft rice paper wraps crisp lettuce, herbs, cucumber, rice vermicelli, and your choice of protein — all dipped in sauces that wake up your palate. They’re satisfying without the post-meal slump, easy to share, and travel well if you’re wandering the waterfront.

  • Light but filling: Fresh herbs and veggies deliver crunch; proteins keep you fueled.
  • Customizable: Pick your filling and sauce for the mood you’re in.
  • Quick: Rolled to order in minutes so you’re back to exploring.
  • Portable: Neat, beach-friendly packaging for strolls and scooter rides.

Quick Cool-Down Game Plan

Order a set of three different rolls for variety, choose two sauces, and pair with a super-cold drink. Start with something bright (like prawn + nước chấm), follow with something cozy (chicken + peanut), and finish with a toasty note (tofu + sesame). Alternate dips to keep each bite interesting.

Top Combos for Beach-Day Cravings

  • Coastal & zesty: Garlic Prawn + Nước Chấm — citrusy, light, and wildly refreshing after sun and salt.
  • Lean & creamy: Poached Chicken & Avocado + Peanut — clean protein with a comforting nutty finish.
  • Plant-powered chill: Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado + Sesame — toasty, balanced, fully vegan.
  • Smoky & bold: Smoked Goose + Sesame — savory depth that still feels beach-appropriate.
  • Sunny comfort: Chicken Satay + Nước Chấm — grilled spice meets bright, tangy dip.

Hydrate & Caffeinate the Right Way

Drinks should cool you down, not weigh you down. Our menu leans crisp and refreshing, with classic Vietnamese phin coffee and no espresso shortcuts.

  • Vietnamese iced coffee (phin): Bold, smooth, and perfect with herb-forward rolls.
  • Salted coffee: Our house favorite — a lightly sweet, cloud-topped cool-down.
  • Coconut coffee: Tropical, creamy, and surprisingly light.
  • Fresh orange or seasonal juices: Naturally hydrating and beach-day friendly.
  • Matcha latte (iced): Earthy, antioxidant-rich, and ultra-sippable.

Fast, Fresh, and Beach-Friendly

Everything’s rolled to order so rice paper stays soft and springy and the herbs keep their snap. Sauces are made in small batches all day for clean, bright flavor. Taking away? We pack neatly so your rolls travel well — whether you’re heading back to the sand, cycling the coast road, or catching a ride across town.

Getting Here from the Beach

We’re in the An Thuong neighborhood, a quick ride or casual walk from the shoreline. The address is 05 An Thượng 6, Bắc Mỹ Phú, Ngũ Hành Sơn — easy to find on maps and close to the main beach roads. If you’re navigating by landmark, think “a few blocks inland from My Khe.” For turn-by-turn directions, use our Google Maps pin.

Best Times to Visit

We’re open Monday to Saturday, 10:00–20:00 (closed Sunday). Early afternoons are the most relaxed for a breezy patio break; evenings draw a friendly post-beach crowd. If you’re short on time, order takeaway — your set will be ready fast and still taste great on the move.

Ordering Tips for Families & Groups

  • Variety wins: Mix three or more fillings and get at least two sauces.
  • Balance the set: Include a seafood option, a lean meat, and a plant-based roll.
  • Share smart: Halve rolls if you want to sample everything without getting too full.
  • Match drinks: One coffee, one juice, one tea covers all preferences and keeps everyone refreshed.

Bottom Line

After a morning at My Khe Beach, you deserve a meal that feels as fresh as the sea. Spring rolls deliver flavor without heaviness, sauces keep things lively, and icy drinks reset your day. Swing by Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang for a quick, feel-good refuel — then get back to the coast, the city, or wherever your Da Nang adventure leads.

Ready to plan your stop? Browse the spring rolls, check out our coffee & matcha, or read more on dipping sauces and how to eat gỏi cuốn like a local. See you soon in An Thuong!
