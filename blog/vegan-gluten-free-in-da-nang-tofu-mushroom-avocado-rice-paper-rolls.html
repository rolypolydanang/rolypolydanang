Vegan & Gluten-Free in Da Nang: Try Our Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado Rice Paper Rolls

August 15, 2025

Finding truly delicious vegan and gluten-free food in Da Nang can be tricky — especially when you also want something light, fresh, and fast. At Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang, our Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado roll checks every box: plant-based, naturally gluten-free, and packed with flavor.

What’s Inside the Roll

  • Silky tofu for protein
  • Umami mushrooms
  • Creamy avocado
  • Crisp herbs & greens for freshness
  • Soft, stretchy rice paper (naturally gluten-free)

House-Made Vegan Sauces

To make your meal even better, pair it with one of our sauces made fresh daily:

  • Peanut sauce (vegan): creamy, nutty, gently sweet.
  • Toasted sesame sauce (vegan): rich, mellow, and aromatic.
  • Nước chấm (gluten-free): tangy, light, and refreshing (contains fish sauce; not vegan).

Note: Peanut and sesame sauces include soy sauce (contains gluten). Ask for nước chấm or request a gluten-free soy alternative if available that day.

Why It Works for Vegan & Gluten-Free Diets

  • Whole-food ingredients — no batter, frying, or heavy dressings.
  • Customizable — choose any sauce, add fresh chili, or go sauce-free.
  • Rolled to order — best texture and maximum freshness.

Perfect Pairings

  • Sesame sauce + Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado → nutty, earthy harmony.
  • Peanut sauce + Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado → comfortingly creamy.
  • Drinks: salted coffee, coconut coffee, matcha latte, or fresh orange juice.

FAQ

Are the tofu rolls gluten-free?

Yes, the roll itself is gluten-free. Choose nước chấm for a gluten-free sauce, or ask about a gluten-free soy alternative.

Is the roll fully vegan?

Yes — the Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado roll is vegan. Just pair it with a vegan sauce (peanut or sesame). Nước chấm contains fish sauce.

How many rolls make a meal?

Most guests find 3–4 rolls plus a drink to be a satisfying lunch.
