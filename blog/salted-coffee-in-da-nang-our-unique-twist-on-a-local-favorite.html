Salted Coffee in Da Nang: Our Unique Twist on a Local Favorite

August 15, 2025

When people think of Vietnamese coffee, they often picture strong, sweet cà phê sữa đá or a rich egg coffee. But here in Da Nang, there’s another specialty worth seeking out — salted coffee (cà phê muối). It’s smooth, creamy, and just a little salty, balancing the deep bitterness of robusta beans with a light, whipped topping.

At Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang, we put our own twist on this local favorite, making it the perfect drink to enjoy alongside a plate of fresh spring rolls or as a treat all on its own.

What Is Salted Coffee?

Salted coffee originated in Huế and quickly became a regional hit. It starts with a base of strong Vietnamese coffee — usually robusta for its intense, chocolatey bitterness. Then we top it with a soft, whipped cream layer that’s lightly salted.

The Result

  • Sweet and salty with a caramel-like richness
  • Creamy but not heavy
  • Balanced — no overwhelming bitterness or sugar rush

When you stir it together, the coffee and cream create a velvety drink that’s unlike any other café beverage.

Our Version at Roly Poly

We use freshly brewed Vietnamese coffee, condensed milk for sweetness, and a house-made salted cream topping that’s whipped to order. It’s served over ice — perfect for Da Nang’s warm weather. While many versions lean heavily on sugar, ours keeps sweetness lower so the coffee still shines.

Perfect Pairings with Fresh Spring Rolls

  • Smoked Goose — the coffee’s richness complements the meat’s depth.
  • Garlic Prawn — the salty-sweet cream balances the shrimp’s natural sweetness.
  • Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado — the creaminess plays off the fresh herbs.

Why Try Salted Coffee in Da Nang?

  • Less sugar for better balance
  • Fresh cream whipped to order
  • Served in a relaxed, friendly café environment in the heart of An Thượng — minutes from Mỹ Khê Beach

FAQ

Is salted coffee very sweet?

No — ours is mildly sweet, so you can taste both the coffee and the cream.

Do you use fresh milk or cream?

We use a whipped cream topping made fresh for each order.

Is it served hot or iced?

Iced only — the chilled coffee and cold cream make it especially refreshing in Da Nang’s warm weather.

At Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang, salted coffee isn’t just a drink — it’s part of the experience. Whether you’re here for a light lunch, a mid-afternoon pick-me-up, or just curious about this unique Vietnamese beverage, we’re ready to pour you a glass.
