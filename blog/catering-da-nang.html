A Food Catering Service in Da Nang That Stays Fresh in the Heat

Fresh spring rolls are naturally crowd-friendly: easy to hold, clean to eat, and built around herbs and vegetables. They feel light in the Da Nang heat, travel well, and work for mixed diets — with vegan and gluten-aware choices clearly labeled. Because we roll to order, the texture stays crisp and the flavor stays clean.

How Our Catering Works

  1. Tell us your headcount and vibe. Casual grazing, structured lunch, or light bites?
  2. Pick your fillings. A balanced mix across seafood, meat, and plant-based keeps everyone happy.
  3. Choose sauces & drinks. Peanut, toasted sesame, and nước chấm — plus juices, coffee, or matcha.
  4. Delivery & setup. We arrive on time with labeled trays and serving tongs; you’re ready in minutes.

Suggested Sets & Portions

As a guide, plan on 2–3 rolls per person for light snacks, or 3–4 for a fuller meal. Here are sample mixes that work well:

  • Classic Mix (balanced): Garlic Prawn, Poached Chicken & Avocado, Lemongrass Beef — sauces: nước chấm, Peanut.
  • Coastal & Light: Garlic Prawn, Poached Chicken & Avocado, Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado — sauces: nước chấm, Sesame.
  • Plant-Forward: Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado + seasonal veg rolls — sauces: Sesame, Peanut.
  • Comfort Option: Satay Chicken, Lemongrass Beef, Soy-Glazed Pork Belly — sauces: Peanut, Sesame.

We label trays clearly and can separate allergens on request. If you’re hosting guests with dietary needs, tell us early and we’ll suggest the safest combinations.

Sauces & Drinks

We prepare sauces in small batches throughout the day so they taste bright and clean. Choose from Peanut (classic & creamy), Toasted Sesame (nutty, vegan), and nước chấm (tangy fish sauce). Drinks that pair well: sugar-free fresh juices, Vietnamese phin coffee, creamy matcha lattes, and our signature Salted Coffee.

Lead Time & Delivery Area

For most events, 24–48 hours’ notice is perfect. For larger groups, reach out earlier so we can reserve prep time. We deliver across An Thuong, My Khe, and central Da Nang — villas, offices, and co-working spaces included.

Serving Tips

  • Shade & cool: Keep trays out of direct sun; air-con or a shaded table is best.
  • Staging: Set sauces front-center with labels; place tongs on each platter.
  • Refills: If your group grazes slowly, we can stagger delivery — just ask.
