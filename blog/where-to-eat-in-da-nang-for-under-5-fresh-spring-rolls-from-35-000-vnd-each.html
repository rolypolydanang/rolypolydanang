Where to Eat in Da Nang for Under $5: Fresh Spring Rolls from 35,000 VND Each

August 15, 2025

Traveling on a budget in Da Nang? You don’t have to settle for greasy fast food. At Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang, our made-to-order gỏi cuốn start at just 35,000 VND per roll — light, flavorful, and minutes from Mỹ Khê Beach.

Budget-Friendly Dining in Da Nang

For under $5 USD, you can enjoy a fresh, satisfying meal without sacrificing quality. Our rolls are generous, packed with herbs and crisp veggies, and paired with house-made sauces.

  • From 35,000 VND each — pick any filling.
  • Rolled to order — best texture and freshness.
  • Customizable — choose your sauce, add fresh chili, go light or rich.

What You Get for 35,000 VND

  • Soft, stretchy rice paper (naturally gluten-free)
  • Fresh herbs, crunchy veg, and rice noodles
  • Your choice of protein or plant-based filling

Build a Full Meal for Under $5

  • 3 rolls + nước chấm (tangy fish sauce) — a light, classic combo
  • 2 rolls + peanut or sesame sauce — richer, still budget-friendly
  • Add a drink — fresh orange juice or Vietnamese iced coffee for a complete set

Customer Favorites

  • Garlic Prawn — bright and aromatic; perfect with nước chấm.
  • Poached Chicken & Avocado — lean and creamy; lovely with peanut sauce.
  • Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado (vegan) — excellent with toasted sesame sauce.
  • Chicken Satay — bold and satisfying; balance with nước chấm.

Tips for Eating Well on a Budget

  1. Mix sauces to keep flavors interesting.
  2. Order 3 different rolls instead of repeats for variety.
  3. Eat fresh — rice paper is best right after rolling.

FAQ

Can I really eat for under $5 in Da Nang?

Yes. Three rolls at 35,000 VND each come to about 105,000 VND — roughly $4–$5 USD depending on exchange rates.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes. Our Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado roll is vegan and naturally gluten-free. Choose nước chấm for a gluten-free sauce.

How fast are the rolls?

They’re rolled to order and typically ready in a few minutes, even at busy times.
