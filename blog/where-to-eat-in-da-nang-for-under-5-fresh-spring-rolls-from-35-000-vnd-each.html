Where to Eat in Da Nang for Under $5: Fresh Spring Rolls from 35,000 VND Each

August 15, 2025 Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang

Traveling on a budget in Da Nang? You don’t have to settle for greasy fast food. At Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang, our made-to-order gỏi cuốn start at just 35,000 VND per roll — light, flavorful, and minutes from My Khe Beach. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or planning a picnic on the sand, you can build a fresh, satisfying meal for under $5 USD (depending on exchange rates).

Why Spring Rolls Are Da Nang’s Best Cheap Eat

Fresh rice paper, bright herbs, crunchy veg, and your choice of protein give you big flavor without heaviness. Because we roll to order, the texture stays supple and the herbs keep their snap. It’s fast, clean, and ideal for hot weather — the definition of a high-value meal.

Transparent Pricing (Mix & Match)

  • Single roll — from 35,000 VND
  • 3 rolls — from 105,000 VND
  • 2 rolls (from 70,000 VND) + Vietnamese coffee (from 30,000 VND)
  • 2 rolls (from 70,000 VND) + fresh juice (from 50,000 VND)
  • 2 rolls (from 70,000 VND) + matcha latte (from 65,000 VND)

Note: Prices shown are starting prices; totals vary by fillings and drink choice. USD equivalents fluctuate with FX.

What You Get for the Price

  • Soft, stretchy rice paper (naturally gluten-free)
  • Fresh herbs, crunchy vegetables, and rice vermicelli
  • Your filling: seafood, chicken, smoked goose, or plant-based
  • House-made sauces packed separately for dine-in or takeaway

Customer Favorites Under $5

  • Garlic Prawn — bright and aromatic; perfect with nước chấm.
  • Poached Chicken & Avocado — lean and creamy; lovely with peanut sauce.
  • Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado (vegan) — earthy and silky; great with toasted sesame.
  • Chicken Satay — bold, satisfying spices; balance with nước chấm.

How Far Will 100k–150k VND Take You?

Keeping it simple? At around 100,000 VND you’re set with 2 rolls + Vietnamese coffee — light, energizing, and well under $5. Want more freshness? 2 rolls + juice from 120,000 VND adds bright acidity that cuts through richer fillings. Feeling plant-based and a little indulgent? 2 rolls + matcha latte from 135,000 VND stays dairy-free and silky (often just over $5 depending on FX). For sharing, a 3-roll mix from 105,000 VND lets you sample seafood, chicken, and vegan in one go.

Diet-Friendly on a Budget

Rice paper and noodles are naturally gluten-free, and most fillings are minimally seasoned. For a fully GF set, choose nước chấm (our Vietnamese fish-sauce dip — it’s gluten-free) and ask us to omit fried shallots on top. Vegan? Pick Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado and a plant-based sauce.

Getting Here & Quick Order Tips

  • Steps from the beach: We’re in An Thuong, ~8–12 minutes on foot from My Khe; search “Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang” on Maps.
  • Order fast: Say your set (“three rolls with nước chấm” or “two rolls and a coffee”) and name sauces upfront; we roll to order so everything stays crisp.
  • Takeaway that travels: We pack sauces on the side so rice paper stays supple — perfect for a beachfront walk or Grab ride.
  • Pay your way: Cash, cards, Apple Pay, and MoMo are accepted.

When to Visit

We’re open Monday to Saturday, 10:00–20:00 (closed Sunday). Late morning and mid-afternoon are relaxed; evenings bring a friendly post-beach crowd. If you’re in a rush, tell us it’s takeaway — we’ll pack for peak freshness.

Bottom Line

Under $5 goes far in Da Nang when you choose fresh. Start at 35,000 VND per roll, build a 3-roll set from 105,000 VND, or pair 2 rolls with a drink for serious value. Fast, flavorful, and a short stroll from the sea.
