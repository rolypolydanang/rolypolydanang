Best Café Near An Thuong: Fresh Spring Rolls & Specialty Drinks at Roly Poly

August 15, 2025 · Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang

If you’re staying in the lively An Thuong neighborhood of Da Nang, you don’t need to wander far for great food and coffee. Just a few minutes from My Khe Beach, Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang is a local favorite where everything is rolled, brewed, and mixed to order. It’s the kind of café you stumble into after a swim and end up returning to for a light lunch, an afternoon pick-me-up, or a relaxed meet-up spot with friends. This guide covers what to expect, what to order, and how to make the most of your visit.

Why Roly Poly Stands Out in An Thuong

Most cafés around An Thuong do solid coffee and pastries; Roly Poly adds a fresh, made-to-order food menu built around Vietnamese gỏi cuốn (fresh spring rolls). The concept is simple: bright herbs, crunchy vegetables, and protein options wrapped in soft rice paper and paired with house-made sauces. The result is a meal that’s light yet satisfying, travel-friendly, and perfect for Da Nang’s warm weather.

The space keeps things easy—come sandy-footed after the beach, grab a table on the breezy patio, and watch rolls being assembled to order. Service is friendly and fast, so you can pop in for a quick bite or linger with a drink while planning your day’s adventures along Nguyễn Văn Thoại and the An Thuong Walking Street.

What’s on the Menu

Fresh Spring Rolls: Pick your protein and we’ll roll it with herbs, lettuce, cucumber, and vermicelli. Popular choices include Garlic Prawn for classic coastal flavors, Beef Lemongrass for something aromatic, Chicken Satay with a gently spiced finish, Poached Chicken & Avocado for a creamy bite, Soy-Glazed Pork Belly for a sweet-savory option, and a full plant-based Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado combo.

House Sauces: Choose from Peanut, Sesame, or classic Nước Chấm. Each is made daily and designed to complement different fillings—nutty with chicken or tofu, tangy-sweet with prawn, and sesame for a toasty depth that flatters vegetables and mushrooms.

Specialty Drinks: Coffee lovers can go traditional with Vietnamese iced coffee or try our silky-light salted coffee. For tea drinkers, our ceremonial-grade matcha lattes come in Classic, Salted Caramel, and Strawberry. There are also fresh, sugar-free juices, ice-cold beers, and soft drinks to keep things cool.

What to Order: Tried-and-True Combos

If it’s your first visit, start with a two-roll set and a drink. The Garlic Prawn + Nước Chấm pairing is a crowd-pleaser—clean, citrusy, and refreshing. Prefer something heartier? Go for Beef Lemongrass + Peanut, where the lemongrass aroma plays nicely with the sauce’s roasted notes. Plant-based? Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado + Sesame gives a satisfying, toasty finish without feeling heavy.

On a hot afternoon, pair your rolls with iced salted coffee for a gentle sweetness and a cloud-like top, or keep it green with a Classic Matcha Latte. If you’re sharing, order a platter with mixed fillings so everyone can sample and find a favorite—just ask the team to include a trio of sauces for dipping.

Dietary & Allergen Notes

Roly Poly is a solid pick for mixed-diet groups. The Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado roll is vegan, and most rolls are naturally made with rice paper and vermicelli. If you’re avoiding gluten, let the team know when you order so they can guide you to the safest options and sauce pairings. As a quick rule of thumb: some proteins (like Beef Lemongrass or Soy-Glazed Pork Belly) and certain sauces may contain gluten. Staff can help with substitutions and recommendations on the spot.

When to Visit & How to Find Us

The café runs Monday to Saturday, 10:00–20:00 (closed Sunday), making it easy to squeeze in a visit before or after the beach. Early afternoons tend to be relaxed; later in the day you’ll see more post-beach crowds rolling in for a light dinner. We’re a short walk from My Khe—search for “Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang” on Google Maps or tap the directions link below for the exact pin.

Staying nearby? Pop in for takeaway on your way to the sand. If you’re working remotely, the patio is a great spot for a productive break with something fresh to eat and a drink that won’t weigh you down.

Value, Speed & Ways to Order

One reason travelers and locals keep us on their An Thuong shortlist is the value: rolls are priced so you can build a balanced meal without overthinking it. Everything is made to order, but the line moves quickly—most guests are served in just a few minutes. You can dine in, grab and go, or order delivery via GrabFood if you’re relaxing at your hotel. Either way, you’ll get the same bright flavors and tidy, travel-friendly packaging.

Before You Go

Roly Poly isn’t just “another café near An Thuong”—it’s a simple idea done with care: fresh rolls, balanced sauces, and drinks that suit the climate. Whether you’re craving a quick bite, need a light lunch between beach sessions, or want a no-fuss spot to meet friends, this is the kind of place that quietly becomes part of your Da Nang routine.

Ready to plan your visit? Browse the spring rolls, check out our coffee & matcha, or jump to our guides on salted coffee and matcha lattes. See you soon near My Khe Beach!
