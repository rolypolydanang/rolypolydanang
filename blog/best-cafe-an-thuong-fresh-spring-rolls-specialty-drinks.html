Best Café Near An Thượng: Fresh Spring Rolls & Specialty Drinks at Roly Poly

August 15, 2025 · Roly Poly Team

If you’re staying in the lively An Thượng neighborhood of Da Nang, you don’t need to wander far for great food and coffee. Just minutes from the beach, Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang is the go-to spot for travelers, expats, and locals looking for something fresh, healthy, and a little different from the usual café fare.

Why Roly Poly is An Thượng’s Hidden Gem

While An Thượng is known for its casual eateries and coffee shops, Roly Poly offers something unique: Vietnamese fresh spring rolls (gỏi cuốn) made to order with vibrant fillings and house-made dipping sauces, plus a curated drink menu that includes Da Nang’s favorite salted coffee and iced matcha lattes.

Our café is a relaxed space to cool down after the beach or enjoy a light meal before exploring the city. We’re also just a short walk from popular An Thượng spots like Nguyễn Văn Thoại and the An Thượng Walking Street, making us easy to find whether you’re on foot or by bike.

What’s on the Menu

Fresh Spring Rolls — Choose from Garlic Prawn, Smoked Goose, Beef Lemongrass, Soy Glazed Pork Belly, Chicken Satay, Poached Chicken & Avocado, or Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado (vegan).

Dipping Sauces — Peanut, Sesame, or Nước Chấm — all made fresh in-house.

Drinks — Salted Coffee, Vietnamese Iced Coffee, Iced Coconut Coffee, Ceremonial Matcha Lattes (Classic, Salted Caramel, Strawberry), fresh juices, ice-cold beer, and soft drinks.

Perfect for Every Occasion

  • Post-beach snack — Our rolls are fresh, light, and portable.
  • Quick lunch — All made to order in minutes.
  • Coffee break — Chill with salted coffee, coconut coffee, or a matcha latte.
  • Group hangouts — Share a platter of rolls with friends.

FAQ

Do you have vegan options?

Yes — try our Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado roll or request peanut or sesame dipping sauce.

Is everything gluten-free?

Most rolls are naturally gluten-free except Beef Lemongrass and Soy Glazed Pork Belly. Peanut and sesame sauces are not gluten-free.

Do you have indoor seating?

We have an open-spaced outdoor patio where you can enjoy the natural breeze — perfect for Da Nang’s laid-back vibe.
