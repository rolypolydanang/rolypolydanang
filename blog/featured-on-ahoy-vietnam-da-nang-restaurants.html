We’re Featured on Ahoy Vietnam: “21 Great Places to Eat in Da Nang”

October 10, 2025 · Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang

We’ve got happy news to share — Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls & Coffee Da Nang has been featured in Ahoy Vietnam’s guide: “21 Great Places to Eat in Da Nang.” It’s a carefully curated list by a local writer who personally visits each spot in the article.

How It Happened

Like many of our new guests, the author first discovered us on the Grab app, enjoyed the food, then came to see us in person at our An Thuong location near My Khe Beach. After trying a few of our rolls in-store, we were added to the roundup — a lovely surprise and a huge motivation for our team.

What They Liked About Roly Poly

  • Mix-and-match fresh rolls: Build your own set and pair with a house sauce.
  • Generous size & feel-good food: A light, hand-held meal that still fills you up.
  • Choices for everyone: Classics like Lemongrass Beef and Satay Chicken, plus Poached Chicken & Avocado. We also offer gluten-free and vegan options.

Those points line up perfectly with what we strive for every day: fresh herbs, crisp veggies, quality proteins, and sauces made in-house.

What This Means to Us — and to You

Being included in a local, experience-driven guide is more than a badge. It helps travelers and neighbors discover a spot where eating well is easy — whether you’re grabbing a quick lunch, fueling a beach day, or meeting friends without the heavy meal.

Plan Your Visit

Find us on Google Maps in the An Thuong area. We’re open Mon–Sat, 10:00–20:00. Dine in, take away, or order delivery on GrabFood.

What to Try First

  • Lemongrass Beef + Peanut sauce — aromatic and satisfying
  • Satay Chicken + Peanut sauce — gently spiced and crowd-pleasing
  • Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado + Sesame sauce — a plant-based favorite

Pair your rolls with a salted coffee or matcha latte and you’re set.

Thank You

Huge thanks to Ahoy Vietnam for visiting and including us — and to every guest who keeps us rolling day after day. See the full guide here: “21 Great Places to Eat in Da Nang.”
