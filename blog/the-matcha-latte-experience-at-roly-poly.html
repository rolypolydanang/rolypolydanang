The Matcha Latte Experience at Roly Poly

August 15, 2025

In Da Nang’s growing café scene, matcha lattes are everywhere — but at Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang, we do things differently. We’re not just serving a pretty green drink; we’re offering the real matcha experience, crafted with ceremonial-grade matcha and made to order by skilled baristas.

Our Three Signature Matcha Lattes

Classic Matcha Latte

Smooth, creamy, and not too sweet — iced matcha with Oatside oat milk, vanilla bean sauce, and first-harvest ceremonial-grade matcha from Shizuoka, Japan. Expect a vibrant green and a clean, gently earthy finish.

Salted Caramel Matcha Latte

A cozy twist: oat milk, salted caramel, and smooth ceremonial matcha from Shizuoka. Sweet-salty balance with umami depth — an addictive comfort drink.

Strawberry Matcha Latte

Creamy matcha topped with a light strawberry cream. Earthy meets fruity — made with oat milk and first-flush matcha for a bright color and silky texture.

Why Shizuoka Ceremonial-Grade Matcha?

We believe matcha should be smooth, vibrant, and naturally sweet. We use small-farm, first-harvest leaves from Shizuoka, stone-ground into an ultra-fine powder to preserve aroma and umami.

To protect quality in Da Nang’s tropical climate, we store matcha in airtight, light-proof containers and keep it cool. Each serving is whisked to order for a velvety texture and frothy top.

Perfect Pairings with Fresh Spring Rolls

  • Classic Matcha Latte → Garlic Prawn; Poached Chicken & Avocado
  • Salted Caramel Matcha Latte → Smoked Goose; Soy-Glazed Pork Belly
  • Strawberry Matcha Latte → Tofu, Mushroom & Avocado; Chicken Satay

These combos balance matcha’s freshness with the light, crisp flavors of our rolls — a refreshing lunch that feels indulgent yet balanced.

FAQ

Is your matcha latte vegan?

Yes. All matcha lattes use Oatside oat milk and contain no dairy.

Hot or iced?

Iced only — perfect for Da Nang’s warm weather.

How sweet are they?

Lightly sweetened: vanilla bean sauce for Classic, salted caramel syrup for Caramel, and fresh strawberry cream for Strawberry.
